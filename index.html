Campus life

MOUNTCARMAL VIDYA-NIKETAN

Mount Carmel Vidyaniketan (MCV) senior secondary school Kottayam was founded in the year 2003with very few students, Now it has matured in to a high quality modern school without sacrificing the unique qualities we have always valued. We are noted for the caring, friendly atmosphere. Our dedicated and experienced staffs encourage the children to work hard, to enjoy their academic subjects, with full part in the wide variety of extracurricular activities on offer small class sizes and closer levels of individual attention enable the children to reach the highest standards.

ART CLASSROOM

To kindle a conducive learning environment on par with the best institutions worldwide, MountCarmal Vidya-Niketan has spared no effort or investment to ensure that the best infrastructure is made available to students to enable them to pursue their education with ease and comfort.

Sports & games

Students are segregated into four houses namely Bluebell House, Rose House, Sunflower House and Shamrock House. Intra-mural games and sports are conducted to promote healthy competitions among the four houses where the adrenaline is gushed up as they take part in all events energetically.
